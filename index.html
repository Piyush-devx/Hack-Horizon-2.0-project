Frequently Asked Questions

Are generic medicines safe?

Yes, generic medicines are required to have the same active ingredients, strength, dosage form, and safety profile as brand-name drugs. They undergo strict quality testing before approval.

Why are they so much cheaper?

Generic medicines don't have the massive research, development, and marketing budgets that brand-name pharmaceutical companies spend. This allows them to offer the same medicine at a fraction of the cost.

Do I need a new prescription?

In most cases, no. Pharmacists can substitute a generic equivalent unless your doctor has specifically written "Dispense as Written" on your prescription. Always consult your healthcare provider if unsure.
